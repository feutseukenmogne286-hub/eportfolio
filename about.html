About Me

I’m a marketing student trained to look at both the numbers and the people behind them. I’ve mapped service encounters using frameworks, audited retail environments to uncover merchandising opportunities, and synthesized market data into actionable plans.

My strengths include secondary research, consumer insight, report writing and presentation design. I enjoy working both independently and in teams, and I’m always looking for ways to make products and services better for customers.

Outside of class, I keep up with trends in retail and food & beverage and love visiting cafés for inspiration.
